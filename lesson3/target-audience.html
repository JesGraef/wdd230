Target Audience

My main target audience are the business owners that are eager to connect and thrive their business in the town of Rothesay, which includes from small to large businesses in the area. Online business owners are also included to have more ideas and participation in the website. But almost anyone in the town of Rothesay are a target audience since everyone encounters with businesses all the time.

Personas

  • My first persona are the new entrepreneurs/small business owners that have fairly new business and need help promoting and growing their network. From ages 18-60 are the perfect age group since they know how to use technology and get the information they need online. This group could be with or without educational background, but are excited and eager to do business. This group can come from any culture, single/married/have families.
  • The second persona are the medium/large businesses who want to maintain and improve their business, and keep a good reputation/image to their community. This is the group that also wants to give back to their community. This can be any age from 18-60, single/married/family, also with or without education but are passionate in doing business. This group should also be tech-savvy and know to use any type of technology.

5 Scenarios

  1. How can I get new business ideas from my competitors?
  2. How can I increase my small group of contacts?
  3. How can I maintain a good reputation for my company?
  4. What are the latest business activities in my town?
  5. How can I promote my business?
